Habibi Code is a community of passionate and value-driven technologists aiming to empower, support, and encourage fellow minorities in tech to further their academic or career goals. Our community is a safe space to get to know and learn from others, ask questions, share advice, attend events, and more.

Events

Habibi Code puts on events on a variety of topics. Some examples of events include:
  • Introduction to Relational Databases
  • Resume Review Workshop
  • Building a Full-Stack Application - Backend and Frontend
  • Q&A Panels with Professionals in Tech
  • From Idea to Product: How to Get Started With Building a Project

Eligibility

If you identify as a minority in tech, you are welcome to join Habibi Code. This includes, but is not limited to: women, BIPOC, the Latinx community, LGBTQ+ individuals, the formerly incarcerated community, those from low socio-economic backgrounds, those with non-traditional educational backgrounds.
